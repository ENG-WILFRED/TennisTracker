# Coach Dashboard - Quick Start Guide

## Overview

Welcome to the Coach Dashboard! This comprehensive system allows tennis coaches to manage their sessions, players, and earnings all in one place.

## Getting Started (First 5 Minutes)

### 1. Access Your Dashboard
- Login as a coach
- Navigate to `/dashboard/coach/[your-id]`
- You'll see the Overview tab with quick stats

### 2. Create Your First Session
1. Click the **Sessions** tab
2. Click **Create Session**
3. Fill in:
   - **Session Title** (e.g., "Serve Technique Training")
   - **Session Type** (1-on-1 or Group)
   - **Start Time** (pick a date and time)
   - **End Time** (when the session ends)
   - **Max Participants** (how many players)
   - **Price** (what you charge per session)
   - **Description** (optional details)
4. Click **Create Session**

### 3. Add Your First Player
1. Click the **Players** tab
2. Click **Add Player** (or wait for players to book)
3. You can:
   - View player profiles
   - Click on a player to add notes
   - Track their session count and status

### 4. Check Your Earnings
1. Click the **Earnings** tab
2. See your:
   - **Available Balance** (ready to withdraw)
   - **Total Earned** (all-time earnings)
   - **Pending Balance** (awaiting confirmation)
3. Request a **Payout** if you have available balance
4. View **Recent Transactions** (ledger)

### 5. Review Your Analytics
1. Click the **Analytics** tab
2. See key metrics:
   - Total Sessions & Completion Rate
   - Player Count (total and active)
   - Revenue & Per-Session Average
   - Player Rating

## Main Features

### 📅 Sessions Tab
**What it does:** Manage all your coaching sessions

**Capabilities:**
- Create 1-on-1 or group sessions
- Set price and max participants
- View bookings for each session
- Cancel or reschedule sessions
- Track session status (scheduled, completed, cancelled)

**Key Metrics:**
- Booked/Available slots
- Session status
- Revenue per session

### 👥 Players Tab
**What it does:** Track all your players

**Capabilities:**
- View all players you're coaching
- Click to open player profile
- Add performance/injury/progress notes
- Track sessions completed
- Manage player status (active/inactive/archived)

**Quick Stats per Player:**
- Sessions completed
- Email address
- Joined date
- Current status

### 💰 Earnings Tab
**What it does:** Manage your money

**Capabilities:**
- View wallet balance
- Request payouts
- Track transaction history
- Choose payment method (Bank Transfer, M-Pesa, Stripe)
- Monitor pending payouts

**Payment Methods:**
- **Bank Transfer** - Wire to your account
- **M-Pesa** - Mobile money (Kenya)
- **Stripe** - Credit card processor

### 📊 Analytics Tab
**What it does:** Understand your coaching business

**Metrics Tracked:**
- **Total Sessions** - All sessions created
- **Completion Rate** - % of sessions completed
- **Total Players** - All players coached
- **Active Players** - Players currently coaching
- **Total Revenue** - All earnings
- **Rating** - Player satisfaction (⭐)

**Insights & Tips:**
- "Improve your completion rate" - if < 80%
- "Build your player base" - if < 5 active
- "Focus on quality" - if rating < 4

### 🗓️ Calendar Tab
**What it does:** Visual view of your schedule

**Features:**
- Monthly calendar
- Sessions shown on calendar days
- Color-coded by status
- Upcoming sessions sidebar
- Navigate between months

### 💬 Messages (Overview Tab)
**What it does:** Stay in touch with players

**Features:**
- Recent conversations
- Quick access to chat
- Last message preview
- Open full chat interface

## Common Tasks

### How to Update a Session
1. Go to **Sessions** tab
2. Click on the session
3. Click **Edit**
4. Change what you need
5. Save

### How to Add Player Notes
1. Go to **Players** tab
2. Click on the player
3. Click **Add Player Note**
4. Choose category (General, Performance, Injury, Progress)
5. Add title and content
6. Click **Save Note**

### How to Request a Payout
1. Go to **Earnings** tab
2. Click **Request Payout**
3. Enter amount (max = Available Balance)
4. Choose payment method
5. Add payment details (account number, etc.)
6. Click **Request Payout**

### How to Message a Player
1. Click **Messages** (on Overview)
2. Click on player conversation
3. Type message
4. Send
5. Or click **Open Messages** for full chat

### How to View Player Performance
1. Go to **Analytics** tab
2. Review overall metrics
3. Go to **Players** tab
4. Click individual player
5. View their notes history

## Smart Features

### Auto-Calculations
- Completion rate calculated from completed sessions
- Per-session revenue = Total Revenue ÷ Completed Sessions
- Player count updates automatically
- Wallet balance updates on book transactions

### Insights System
The dashboard gives you recommendations:
- "Improve completion rate" - Focus on finishing sessions
- "Build your player base" - Offer promotional sessions
- "Focus on quality" - Player satisfaction is low

### Transaction Ledger
Every earning is recorded:
- Session booking, completion, and payment
- Payout requests and processing
- Platform fees charged
- Always shows balance before and after

## Payment Integration

### How Earnings Work
1. **Player Books** → Amount calculated
2. **Session Completes** → Amount credited to wallet
3. **Platform Fee** → Automatic deduction (~10-20%)
4. **Your Earnings** → Available in wallet
5. **Request Payout** → With your payment method
6. **Processing** → Sent to your account in 1-3 days

### Supported Currencies
- USD
- KES (Kenya Shillings)
- More added upon request

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Session not showing up"
**Solution:** 
- Check the date/time is correct
- Ensure you're in the right month
- Refresh the page

### Problem: "Can't request payout"
**Solution:**
- Check Available Balance > Payout Amount
- Ensure payment details are entered
- Contact support if still having issues

### Problem: "Missing player"
**Solution:**
- Check player hasn't been archived
- Search in the player list
- Ensure coach-player relationship exists

### Problem: "Wrong balance"
**Solution:**
- Check recent transactions
- Ensure pending payouts subtracted
- Pending sessions may not be counted

**Q: Can I teach offline and log sessions here?**
A: Yes! Create sessions, record completion, and manage payments manually.

**Q: What if a player cancels last minute?**
A: Mark the booking as cancelled, and wallet remains unchanged.

A: Not yet, but coming in next update. For now, create manually each week.
